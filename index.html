About Us

Welcome To Olivia's Kitchen Restaurant

Olivia's Kitchen is situated in Auckland's Albany, North Shore, on the busy Oteha Valley Road. Here at Olivia's we are dedicated to bringing you the freshest and tastiest deep fried appetisers,burgers and classic Chinese Takeaways as well as popular South East Asian stir-fried dishes.

We are devoted to animal welfare and sustainable farming, and proud to announce we are the first Asian Takeaway to go full free range for all our meats and eggs.

Our belief is that there should be accountability and traceability in everything we eat, that's why we only use locally sourced certified free range farmed pork, poultry and eggs so you can be sure to enjoy our food with a peace of mind.

our Suppliers

Pork & Bacon

Certified free range pork and bacon proudly supplied by Freedom Farms. The pork is farmed right here in New Zealand with approval from the Royal New Zealand SPCA Blue Tick Standard, guaranteed fresh and 100% hormone free.

Chicken

We use Tegal NZ's new line of free range poultry 'Good Life' fresh whole chickens and chicken breasts distributed through The Fresh Chicken Company. The chicken is guaranteed to meet Halal compliance and farming practices are approved by the Royal New Zealand SPCA Blue Tick Standard.

Eggs

Our free range eggs are freshly delivered to us directly from the local certified free range small cottage type EcoEgg farm.

Beef

We use 100% New Zealand Genuine Angus lean beef from one of NZ's biggest meat processors, Affco NZ, delivered to us fresh by JR Wholesale.
